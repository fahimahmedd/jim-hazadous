Founded in 2019, Jim's Hazardous Material Removal (Auburn) began with a simple mission: make hazardous material remediation safe, transparent, and accessible. What started as a small team of two certified asbestos removers in a modest Sydney garage has grown into the city's most trusted remediation and demoliton company.

Our approach is built on a foundation of diligence and expertise. We utilize a Systematic After-Method, a meticulous step-by-step process designed to ensure that every project is handled with the highest level of technical precision. We believe that true professionalism is proven through documentation. Therefore, we maintain detailed records of every stage of the job, ensuring full traceability and accountability. By keeping all lines of communication open, we ensure you are never left in the dark, fostering a transparent partnership that prioritizes your peace of mind
