The Removal Process:

Depending on the scale of the job the removal will take place over various periods of time and may include the use of various equiptments or lifters for safe work at heights. Removal usually begins by removing trims and fastenings to expose the asbestos-containing material. The materials will be carefully removed and relocated to the disposal areas where they will be double bagged and wrapped as well as labelled prior to disposal.
