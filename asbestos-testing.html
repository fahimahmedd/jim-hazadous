Working out what needs to be removed and when is the first major step in identifying asbestos containing materials around the home or place of work. A visual inspection of the presence of asbestos containing materials is conducted by Jim's Hazardous Material Removal specialists.

Jim's Hazardous Materials manages the asbestos testing process on your behalf in conjunction with a NATA approved laboratory. Professional testing undertaken by licensed asbestos assessors is the best and safest way to determine whether or not a property contains asbestos materials.
