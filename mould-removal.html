How Our Process Works

01

Inspection & Testing

We begin with a comprehensive assessment using moisture meters and thermal imaging to identify all affected areas – even mould hidden behind walls or under flooring.

02

Containment & Filtration

We establish negative pressure zones with physical barriers and use HEPA air scrubbers to capture airborne spores, preventing cross-contamination during removal.

03

Removal & Treatment

Using EPA-approved antimicrobial treatments, we carefully remove all mould colonies and treat affected materials while addressing the underlying moisture source.

04

Clearance Testing

Independent post-remediation verification testing confirms the space is safe and mould-free, with detailed documentation provided.
