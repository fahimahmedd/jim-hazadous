01 philosophy

See it before it starts

Mould doesn't appear overnight – it develops over weeks and months. Our prevention work is about spotting the early warning signs and addressing conditions before mould has a chance to establish.

The prevention framework

A

Assess

We evaluate your property's unique risk factors – from construction type to lifestyle patterns.

B

Block

We identify and seal entry points for moisture – the single most important factor in mould prevention.

C

Control

We implement moisture control solutions – ventilation, dehumidification, and drainage improvements.

D

Defend

We apply protective barriers in high-risk zones and establish monitoring protocols.
